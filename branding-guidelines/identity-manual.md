## 📐 Logo & Asset Refinement Strategy

[cite_start]All digital design deliverables must honor the historic roots of our institution while scaling for the future[cite: 26]. 

### 1. Logo Refinement Rules
* [cite_start]**Heritage Integration:** Refinements must be modeled directly on the foundational SLIATE and HNDIT identity structures[cite: 15].
* [cite_start]**Theme Elements:** Integrate minimal, futuristic design elements inspired by networking nodes or cybersecurity motifs[cite: 19].
* [cite_start]**Format Standards:** All final assets must be stored in editable source files and export-ready formats (`.svg` or transparent `.png`)[cite: 19].

### 2. Forbidden Graphic Alterations
* [cite_start]❌ Do not use unapproved or legacy shades of blue or gold outside of `#14213D` and `#FBBF24`[cite: 9, 26].
* [cite_start]❌ Do not compress, stretch, or alter the structural dimensions of the institution's symbols[cite: 26].
* [cite_start]❌ Do not use non-responsive raster formats for live web application headers[cite: 19].
